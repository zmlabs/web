# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2025-09-23_  
_App: Volume HUD_

We respect your privacy. This policy explains our approach to data handling.

---

## 1. Data We Process

**None.** Volume HUD operates entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information.

| Data Category            | Collection      | Storage        | Transmission      |
| ------------------------ | --------------- | -------------- | ----------------- |
| **Personal Information** | Never collected | Not applicable | Never transmitted |
| **Usage Data**           | Never collected | Not applicable | Never transmitted |
| **Audio Data**           | Never accessed  | Not applicable | Never transmitted |
| **System Information**   | Never collected | Not applicable | Never transmitted |
| **Device Identifiers**   | Never collected | Not applicable | Never transmitted |

---

## 2. Network Activity

Volume HUD **does not** connect to the internet or any external services. The app functions entirely offline and requires no network permissions.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Volume HUD does **not** use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

---

## 4. App Functionality

Volume HUD:

- Displays a custom user interface for system volume controls
- Replaces the default macOS volume feedback UI
- Operates entirely within the macOS sandbox
- Requires no special permissions or authorizations
- Does not access, process, or store any audio content
- Only reads system volume level information for display purposes

---

## 5. Data Access and Security

- The app only accesses publicly available system volume level information
- No audio content, files, or personal data is accessed or processed
- All functionality operates locally on your device
- No data leaves your device through this application
- The app follows macOS sandbox security guidelines
- No persistent data storage occurs
- No third-party libraries or frameworks are used

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Since Volume HUD does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. Your continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
