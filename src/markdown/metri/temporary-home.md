# Metri

A professional, high-precision metronome app designed for modern iOS.

## Key Features

- **Liquid Glass Design**: A stunning, modern interface featuring authentic glass materials and refined visual effects.
- **High Precision Timing**: Rock-solid audio engine ensures perfect timing for your practice.
- **Visualizer**: Responsive beat visualization to help you stay on tempo visually.
- **Privacy Focused**: No tracking, no analytics, no data collection. Your practice is your business.
- **Customizable**: Choose from multiple high-quality sound packs and time signatures.

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0+
